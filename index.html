About Me

I am a PhD researcher specializing in Geodesy, GNSS, and Seismo-Geodesy, with a particular focus on the analysis of ionospheric disturbances as earthquake precursors. My research integrates GNSS observations, multi-source satellite data, and artificial intelligence techniques to improve earthquake monitoring and prediction frameworks.

Research Interests

  • GNSS data processing and ionospheric monitoring
  • Seismo-geodesy and earthquake precursor analysis
  • Integration of GNSS, InSAR, and seismic catalog data
  • Artificial intelligence in geosciences
