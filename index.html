About me

Hello! I am Layachi Abdelkebir, a PhD student in Geodesy at the National Higher School of Geodesy and Space Techniques (ENSGTS), Algeria. My research focuses on GNSS, ionospheric seismology, and earthquake precursors, with a particular interest in the integration of multi-source satellite data and artificial intelligence for seismic hazard monitoring and mitigation.

I am currently working on advanced methodologies for detecting ionospheric disturbances associated with seismic activity, combining GNSS, InSAR, and seismic catalog data within hybrid AI-based frameworks.
