About Me

I am a PhD researcher specializing in Geodesy, GNSS, and Ionospheric Seismology, with a focus on analyzing ionospheric disturbances for earthquake prediction through the integration of GNSS and multi-source satellite data. My research aims to develop advanced models for natural hazard monitoring and mitigation.

Research Interests

  • GNSS data processing and ionospheric monitoring
  • Earthquake prediction and seismic precursor analysis
  • Integration of GNSS, InSAR, and satellite-based geophysical data
  • Artificial Intelligence and machine learning applications in geoscience

Publications & Projects

  • SeismoAI-QSS: A hybrid AI-based framework for earthquake prediction using GNSS, InSAR, and seismic catalog data.
  • Ionospheric Disturbance Analysis: Multi-source satellite data integration for early earthquake detection.
  • Several contributions presented at international conferences (IAG, EGU, etc.).
